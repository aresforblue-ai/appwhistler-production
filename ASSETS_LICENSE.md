## Logo and Brand Usage Guidelines

### Logo Watermarking (Recommended)

**For Figma prototypes:**

1. **Add watermark layer:**
   - Semi-transparent "¬© AppWhistler" text
   - Position in corner of all artboards
   - 10-20% opacity

2. **Export with watermark:**
   - Keep watermark visible in shared prototypes
   - Prevents unauthorized use
   - Shows active brand protection

3. **Remove for production:**
   - Clean exports for official releases
   - Documented in design system

### Logo Variations

We provide these logo files:

- `logo.svg` ‚Äî Full color logo
- `logo-dark.svg` ‚Äî Dark mode variant
- `logo-mono.svg` ‚Äî Monochrome version
- `logo-icon.svg` ‚Äî Icon only (no text)

**Do not:**
- Modify logo proportions or colors
- Add effects (shadows, glows, etc.)
- Use low-resolution versions
- Combine with other logos

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I use AppWhistler designs in my portfolio?

**A:** Yes! Contributors can showcase their work on AppWhistler in portfolios, with attribution and context that it's an open source project.

### Q: Can I use screenshots in a paid course?

**A:** No, that's commercial use. Contact us for a commercial license.

### Q: Can I create a paid theme based on AppWhistler's design?

**A:** No, but you can create a free theme (with attribution) or request commercial licensing.

### Q: Can I print AppWhistler screenshots in a book?

**A:** For educational/academic books, yes (with attribution). For commercial books, contact us.

### Q: What if I'm making a fork for commercial use?

**A:** You must fully rebrand (remove all AppWhistler assets) per our [CLA.md](CLA.md). The CC BY-NC license protects our visual identity.

### Q: Can I use the color scheme in my project?

**A:** Color schemes are not copyrightable. You can use similar colors, but don't copy our complete design system for commercial purposes.

### Q: Is the glassmorphism effect protected?

**A:** The effect itself is not protected (it's a common design trend). Our specific implementation and combinations are protected under this license.
